Discord Economy Bot

Greed Bot

currency that doesn't pretend to fix anything

A server economy built around three currencies, daily rituals, and a prestige system. No shop. No leaderboards. Just small habits and a reason to show up.

The Economy

Three Currencies

Every user has a wallet with three distinct currencies. Each one is earned differently and serves a different purpose.

🪨
Obsidian Shards

The main currency. Earned through daily check-ins, tasks, and gifted from other members. VIP members earn double from dailies.

🔥
Umbral Prisms

Secondary currency earned through engagement tasks. Required alongside Obsidian when forging Veils.

🌑
Nightglass Cores

Prestige currency. You can't earn these directly — they have to be forged. Rare on purpose. Can't be gifted.

Commands

Economy

All economy commands are ephemeral — only you can see the response.

/balance Check your current wallet. Shows all three currencies at a glance.
/daily Claim your daily reward. Shows your current streak and how much you earned. Resets at midnight UTC. VIP members get double Obsidian.
/earn See every way to earn currency — your daily status, all available tasks with cooldowns, and how to forge Veils.
/task complete Complete a task by its ID (shown in /earn). Tasks have cooldowns and reset automatically — repeatable ones show up again once the timer is up.
/gift create Deduct currency from your wallet and create a claimable gift with an optional message. Works with Obsidian or Ember — not Veils.
/gift claim Claim a gift by its ID. Each gift can only be claimed once, and you can't claim your own.
/forge Trade 50 Obsidian + 50 Ember for 1 Nightglass Core. Shows you what you have and what you're missing before the forge goes through.

Earning

Tasks

Tasks are small recurring actions that reward you for showing up. They're themed around the server and reset on their own cooldowns.

🕯️ checkin Daily check-in — earns 2 Obsidian every 24 hours.
📓 journal Write a short journal entry — earns 4 Obsidian every 24 hours.
🌑 grounding Complete a grounding exercise — earns 1 Umbral Prism every 12 hours.
🔥 streak3 3-day visit streak bonus — earns 8 Obsidian every 72 hours.
🩸 ember5 Deep engagement over 5 days — earns 3 Umbral Prisms every 120 hours.
Task completion is on the honor system. Complete them with /task complete <id> when you've actually done the thing.

Staff

Moderation

All moderation commands live under /mod and are restricted to members with ban, kick, or manage roles permissions. Actions are logged and DM the target where possible.

/mod ban

Ban a member with an optional reason and message history deletion.

/mod massban

Ban up to 20 users at once by ID or mention.

/mod kick

Kick a member. Checks role hierarchy before acting.

/mod masskick

Kick up to 20 members at once by ID or mention.

/mod mute

Apply the mute role to a member. Requires MUTE_ROLE_ID in your .env.

/mod massmute

Mute up to 20 members at once.

/mod hackban

Pre-emptively ban a user by ID — they don't need to be in the server.

Mass actions include a short rate-limit buffer between each user to avoid hitting Discord's API limits. Results show a breakdown of who succeeded and who didn't.
